My Story

Growing up in an immigrant household instilled hard work from a young age. From academics to a variety of extracurriculars, overachieving had always been expected. However, I fell in love with programming toward the end of my high school career because of it's ability to challenge me. The thrill of solving difficult problems or program an app inspired me to follow in the footsteps of my mother and brother into the tech industry.

My Inspiration

I was fortunate enough to grow up around a lot of opportunities while living in San Francisco. It was destined for me to pursue this career path with all my guidance. However, many other minority children children are not exposed to the same possibilities. I want to use my experience as a chance to unveil the tech industry to those in my Latino community. I believe exposing them to this field will lead to a more productive and diverse industry which will continue to flourish.

My goals

Make An Impact

Tech has become such an influential part of our every day lives. I dream to be part of the innovative culture that impacts our current lives and future of society.

Team Development

Working in collaboration is crucial to a team's success and development. I will thrive in group situations with my ability to communicate intelligently and mediate disputes.

Inspire Community

I have always loved helping people considering programming because it is oozing with opportunity. I want to expose those in my community to the same curiosity I have for this field.
